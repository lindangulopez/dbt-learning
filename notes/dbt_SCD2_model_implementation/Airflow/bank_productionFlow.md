# Production Workflow Notes: Bank Reconciliation Documentation Purpose

## Objective

Maintain complete and accurate bank reconciliation documentation to support reliable cash reporting, operational controls, and audit readiness.

## Documentation Requirements

* Record reconciliation details for each accounting period.
* Maintain supporting evidence for all adjustments, discrepancies, and reconciling items.
* Document explanations for outstanding transactions, corrections, and account differences.
* Preserve reconciliation history for review, audit, and compliance purposes.

## Control Benefits

* Provides a clear audit trail for cash activity.
* Supports verification of reported cash balances.
* Enables timely investigation of errors, unusual transactions, and potential fraud indicators.
* Demonstrates consistent application of financial control procedures.

## Operational Standard

All bank reconciliations must include sufficient documentation to explain differences between bank records and accounting records and allow independent review of the reconciliation process.
